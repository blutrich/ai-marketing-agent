## Primary CTAs (Most Used)

### Action-Oriented
- "Start building"
- "Try it now"
- "Ship your first app"
- "Build something today"

### Invitation
- "Join the builders"
- "See what's possible"
- "What will you build?"

## Content-Type CTAs

### Feature Announcements
- "Try it now"
- "Available today"
- "Ship something with it"

### Success Stories
- "Read the full story"
- "What will you build?"
- "Their story could be yours"

### Demos/Videos
- "Watch the demo"
- "See it in action"
- "Full video: [link]"

### Engagement
- "What would you build first?"
- "Drop your app below"
- "What should I build next?"

---

## CTA Patterns

### Don't
- "Click here to learn more"
- "Visit our website"
- Generic "Learn more"

### Do
- Action verbs: Start, Build, Ship, Try, Create
- Second person: "your first app", "you can"
- Immediate: "today", "now", "right now"
